Did you know that podcast means a digital audio or video file made available on the Internet for downloading to a computer or mobile device, typically available as a series, new installments. For apple's lovers, the word podcast comes from "iPod" and "broadcast". Thus, the files distributed are typically in audio or video formats, but may sometimes include other file formats such as PDF or ePub. The term was invented by BBC journalist Ben Hammersley in 2004, other names for podcasting include "net cast".
